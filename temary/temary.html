Used to - Be Used to - Get Used to

Learn how to talk about past habits

This section teaches you how to use "Used to", "Be Used to", and "Get Used to" to talk about stuff <> you used to do or things you’re getting used to now. Wanna sound cooler and more natural in English? Then this is for you!

Prepositions with time

Get the hang of time prepositions

Let’s dive into prepositions like during, since, until, and for. Mastering these will totally level up your game when talking about how long stuff has been happening or when things started.

Second Conditional

Talk about “what ifs”

Here you’ll learn the Second Conditional to talk about what could happen if things were different. Like, “If I had a million dollars, I would buy a castle!” Sounds fun, right?
