Used to - Be Used to - Get Used to

Learn how to express past habits

In this section, you will learn how to use "Used to", "Be Used to", and "Get Used to" to talk about past habits, routines, and states. Understanding these expressions will help you describe changes and experiences in a more natural way in English.

Prepositions with time

Master time expressions in English

In this section, you will learn how to use prepositions like during, since ,until , and for. Mastering these prepositions will allow you to talk about periods of time, when actions started, and how long something has lasted, all in a more accurate and natural way in English.

Second Conditional

Talk about hypothetical situations

Learn how to use the Second Conditional to express hypothetical situations, future possibilities, and dreams. Understanding this structure will allow you to speak about things that are not real but could happen under certain conditions, making your English more expressive and versatile.
